Warehouse rules

Colours system

Shift Leader

is an experienced professional responsible for organizing work and monitoring the performance of tasks in his subordinate units. Authorized to change the tasks of the employee, as well as to transfer the employee to another process. Is a person who makes final decisions in his unit.

Team leader

is a professional responsible for leading, monitoring and managing a group of employees.In addition, he gives instructions, corrects the work, helps to solve the employee's work problems.

Trainer

is a highly qualified specialist whose main task is to train and prepare new employees for independent work in the warehouse. Authorized to admit or not admit a new employee to the warehouse.

Controler

is a highly qualified specialist whose main task is the control of goods prepared for shipment, as well as the control of the warehouse workers work process.

Warehouse worker

is an operator for processing returned parcels, whose main task is checking, cleaning and repackaging goods, as well as accounting for these goods in a computer program.
